Contrast

CT Moving And Storage

This site uses the design principle contrast very well. The viberant orange color lays against the white creates a great pop of the color. The black accent coloring makes text lines very easy to read. This site as a whole is a prime example of contrast done right on a "simple" basis.

Alignment

CT Moving And Storage

This site has a bunch of cards on this page. Filled with text and icons. Each on of these cards are aligned to one another along with the icons and text in each card. This creates a seemless experience for the user of the site.

White Space
And
Clean Design

CT Moving And Storage

This sites home page is a great example of a clean design with white space. You have the white nav bar, good white space around text areas, white spacing between sections, white spacing between colored elements, etc. Having these white spaces create balance for the site and allow your eye time to rest and not be over stimulated by the site as a whole.
